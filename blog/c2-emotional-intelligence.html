What learnings did you take from the chapter?

I learnt that there were many benefits to be gained from mindfulness training, both mentally and physically -interesting that those who received mindfulness training heal quicker and or are healthier.

I gained an understand of how go about practicing mindfulness and will attempt to integrate this into a part of my day.

Most importantly, I learnt that you can never use too many analogys. More the better.

I will be the mountain that stays on the bike, so that I too can enjoy a clear snow-globe.

Challenging. I have a wandering mind.

My mind is like a child that can't sit still because they had too much screen time as a baby.
