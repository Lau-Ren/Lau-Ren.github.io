Self Confidence

27.03.2016

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self-awareness, as decribed in SIY, seems to be related to mindfulness, in that the sense of awareness is focused on your own internal state.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I went for the body scan and found it quite relaxing, it made me sleepy.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not me" section?

That emotions should be thought of as being seperate from ourselves. We are not our emotions, rather our emotions are simply physiological experiences that happen to the body
